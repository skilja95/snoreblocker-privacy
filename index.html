Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

SnoreBlocker is designed with privacy at its core. Audio is analyzed on-device in real time and is never recorded, stored, or uploaded anywhere.

1. Information We Collect

SnoreBlocker does not collect personal information. The app operates entirely on your device.

  • Microphone audio — analyzed in real time to detect snoring. Audio is never saved to disk or transmitted.
  • Sleep session data — stored locally on your device only (date, duration, snoring episodes, sleep score). This data never leaves your phone.
  • Subscription status — managed by Apple via StoreKit. We do not have access to your payment information.

2. Microphone Usage

SnoreBlocker requests access to your iPhone microphone solely to detect snoring sounds during sleep monitoring sessions. Audio processing happens entirely on your device using Core ML and AVFoundation frameworks.

  • Audio is never recorded or saved
  • Audio is never transmitted to any server
  • Audio processing stops immediately when you end a session

3. Data Storage

All sleep data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This includes:

  • Session dates and durations
  • Snoring episode counts
  • Sleep quality scores
  • Hourly snoring patterns

This data is never uploaded to any server. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed from your device.

4. Apple Watch

SnoreBlocker communicates with Apple Watch using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework. This communication happens locally between your iPhone and Apple Watch over Bluetooth and Wi-Fi. No data is sent to external servers.

On Apple Watch, SnoreBlocker requests HealthKit access solely to enable background operation during sleep sessions using a workout session. No health data is read, written, or shared.

5. Subscriptions & Payments

Subscriptions are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, process, or store any payment or billing information. All subscription management is done through your Apple ID and App Store settings.

6. Third-Party Services

SnoreBlocker does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share any data with third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

SnoreBlocker is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
